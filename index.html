Maize Starch,
Food Grade

A fine, white or slightly yellowish, odorless powder processed from corn (maize). It is processed to meet strict safety and quality standards for human consumption. Food grade maize starch is widely used as a thickener, texturizer, binder, and anti-caking agent in various food products.

Maize Starch,
Industrial Grade

A fine, white or slightly yellowish, odorless powder processed from corn (maize) that is specifically produced for non-food applications. It has high starch content and undergoes minimal processing to maintain functionality for industrial uses. This makes it ideal for thickening, binding, and adhesive applications in various sectors like textiles, paper, paint, and adhesives.
